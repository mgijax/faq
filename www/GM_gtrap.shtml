How do I search for all gene trap cell lines associated with a gene or marker?

  1. Click here to go to the Phenotypes and Alleles Query Form. The Phenotypes and Alleles Query Form is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. Find the Nomenclature & location section of the form and type Nrp2 in the Gene/Marker text box.

  3. In the Categories section in the lower half of the page, check the box next to gene trapped located at the bottom of the center column.

  4. Click the Search button.

  5. Click any allele symbol in the first column of the search results to view detail about an allele.
