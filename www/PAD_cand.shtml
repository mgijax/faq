How do I find candidate genes for a given phenotype and a specific genome location?

  1. Click here to go to the Genes and Markers Query Form. The Genes and Markers Query Form is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. Select Gene from the Type drop-down menu in the first section of the form.

  3. Select chromosome 7 in the Map position section of the form.

  4. In the text box beneath Genome Coordinates, type 130 and 140.

  5. Change the units from bp to Mbp in the drop-down menu near the Genome Coordinates text box.

  6. Find the Mouse phenotypes & mouse models of human disease section in the lower half of the form.

  7. Click the link to Anatomical Systems Affected by Phenotypes beneath the text box.

  8. Select homeostasis/metabolism from the menu in the new window.

  9. Click the Add to Query button.

  10. Click the Search button.
