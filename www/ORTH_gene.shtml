How do I find the mouse ortholog of a human gene?
Overview
You can use the Mammalian Orthology Query Form to search for orthologies between two or more mammalian species.
Accessing the Mammalian Orthology Query Form
On most MGI pages, you can access the Mammalian Orthology Query Form from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the searches/homology_form.shtml' onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('searches/homology_form.shtml'); return false;"> Mammalian Orthology Query Form in a new window.


Example 1. Finding the mouse ortholog of human MYO6
  1. Select human (Homo sapiens) from the Primary Species pick list.

  2. Type myo6 into the Marker Symbol/Name text box.

  3. Select mouse, laboratory (Mus musculus domesticus) from the Comparison Species pick list.

  4. Click Search.
  1. The search results display a table including genomic location, accession identifiers, and evidence codes for human-mouse orthologs of gene MYO6.

  2. To download all human and mouse orthologs, visit the MGI Data and Statistical Reports page located beneath the Download menu.
