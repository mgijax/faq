How do I find the mouse ortholog of a human gene?

  1. Click here to go to the Mammalian Orthology Query Form. The Mammalian Orthology Query Form is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. On the Mammalian Orthology Query Form, select human (Homo sapiens) from the Primary Species list.

  3. Type Myo6 into the Marker Symbol/Name text box.

  4. Select mouse from the Comparison Species list.

  5. Click the Search button.

  6. The search results display a table including genomic location, accession identifiers, and evidence codes for human-mouse orthologs of gene Myo6.

  7. To download all human and mouse orthologs, visit the Data Reports page located beneath the Download menu.
