How do I view a comparative map of mouse and human chromosomes?

  1. Click here to open the Mouse-Human Orthology Map in a new window. The Mouse-Human Orthology Map is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. This map shows all 20 mouse chromosomes in black, and orthologous human chromosomes as colored histograms. Each histogram represents a segment of a human chromosome. The length of the bar in a histogram indicates the number of human/mouse orthologies in that region of the mouse chromosome.

  3. To retrieve a detailed orthology map for a mouse chromosome, click on a mouse chromosome.

  4. Alternatively, click on a colored histogram to retrieve a compact tabular listing of all the human/mouse orthologies for that pair of chromosomes.
