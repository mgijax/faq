How do I display my mouse gene expression data in the context of mouse pathways?

  1. Click here to go to the Biochemical Pathways page. This page may be accessed by clicking the Biochemical Pathways link on the MGI home page, or by clicking the tab near the top center of other MGI pages.

  2. Find the box labeled What can I do here? located at left. Click the link to Overlay experimental data onto the Pathway Tools Omics Viewer near the bottom of this box.

  3. Find the pull-down menu next to Select a dataset located at right. Choose Mus musculus C57BL/6J from this menu.

  4. To upload a file, click the Choose file button. This file should be tab-delimited text with gene symbols in the first column and expression level in the second and all subsequent columns.

  5. Scroll down the page to the Single Experiment Time Step or Animated Time Series section. Find the box entitled Data column (numerator in ratios). Type the number 1 in this box.

  6. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Submit button.
