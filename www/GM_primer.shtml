How do I find primer sequence for a gene?

  1. Click here to open the Molecular Probes and Clones Query Form in a new window. The Molecular Probes and Clones Query Form is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. For Gene/Marker Symbol/Name, type Myo6 in the text box.

  3. Find the Probe/Clone attributes section. Select "primer" from the Sequence Type drop-down menu.

  4. Click the Search button.

  5. Click the link to MGI:6655 in the Nucleotide Probe/Clone AccID column to view details about primer sequences, alleles, fragment sizes and strains.

  6. To find genotyping protocols including reaction components, cycling conditions and primers, visit JAX Mice at http://jaxmice.jax.org.

  7. In the left hand navigation bar, click the link to Mouse Strain Information beneath JAX Mice & Services.

  8. In the lower right of the page, find the JAX Mice Resources section and click the link to Genotyping Protocols near the bottom.

  9. Type Tub in the Gene Symbol text box and click the Search button.

  10. Click a link in the Protocol column for detailed information.

  11. Scroll down the page below the gel image to view detailed reaction conditions.

  12. For additional information please contact JAX Mice directly at micetech@jax.org or 800-422-MICE.
