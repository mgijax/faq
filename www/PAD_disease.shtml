How do I find mouse models for a particular disease?

  1. Click WIFetch?page=omimVocab&subset=A"TARGET = "BLANK">here to go to the Human Disease Vocabulary Browser. The Human Disease Vocabulary Browser is located beneath the Search menu in the toolbar, as shown below.

  2. As an example, type Huntington in the text box.

  3. Click the Search button.

  4. Click the link to Huntington Disease; HD from the list of results to view details about the mouse models associated with this disease.
