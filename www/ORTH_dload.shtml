How do I download all mouse and human orthologs?

  1. Click here to go to the Data Reports page. The Data Reports page is located beneath the Download menu as shown below.

  2. Click the link to Mammalian Orthology to move down the page.

  3. Click the link to HMD_HumanSequence.rpt to download a plain text file containing all orthologies between mouse and human along with sequence identifiers for each species.
