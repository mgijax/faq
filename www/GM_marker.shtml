How do I search for genes, QTL, or other features in a specific genome interval?

Overview
You can use the Genes and Markers Query Form to narrow your search to a specific region of the mouse genome. Within this interval, you can choose to retrieve genes, QTL, pseudogenes, microRNA, or other marker types.
Accessing the Genes and Markers Query Form
On most MGI pages, you can access the Genes and Markers Query Form from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the Genes and Markers Query Form in a new window or tab.


Example 1. Finding genes on Chromosome 7 between 130 and 140 Mbp
  1. Select Gene from the Type drop-down menu.

  2. Select 7 from the Chromosome(s) list.

  3. Type 130 - 140 in the text box beneath Genome Coordinates.

  4. Change bp to Mbp in the drop-down menu near Genome Coordinates.

  5. Click Search.

Example 2. Finding QTL on Chromosome 7 between 130 and 140 Mbp
  1. Follow the steps detailed in Example 1 above, but select QTL instead of Gene for step 1.

  2. Click the Search button.
