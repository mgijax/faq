How do I find existing recombinase-expressing transgenes/knock-ins that have a given promoter (driver)?
Overview
You can use the tools listed under "Access Data" to find transgenes that express recombinase.
Find Alleles
On most MGI pages, you can access the Recombinase (cre) Specificity page from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the recombinase.shtml' onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('recombinase.shtml'); return false;"> Recombinase (cre) Specificity page in a new window. Scroll down this page for further instructions. The "Find Alleles" section allows you to search for in a specified tissue or under the control of a given gene.


Example 1. Finding a transgene expressing recombinase under the control of Nes.
  1. Select Nes From the Recombinase driven by selection list.

  2. Click Go.
  1. In the summary page that appears, click an allele symbol to go to the Transgene Detail page for more information.
  1. On the Transgene Detail page, go to the Recombinase specificity section and click one of the anatomical systems for more information.
  1. This returns a page summarizing recombinase expression under the control of Nes in the cardiovascular system.
