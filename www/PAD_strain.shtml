How do I find available strains carrying a mutation in a specific gene?

  1. Click here to open the International Mouse Strain Resource (IMSR) search form in a new window. The IMSR is located in the toolbar, as shown below.

  2. In the Gene or Allele Symbol/Name text box, type pax.

  3. Click the Search button.

  4. The International Mouse Strain Resource contains information from a number of different repositories including the European Mouse Mutant Archive (EM), The Jackson Laboratory (JAX), and the RIKEN BioResource Center (BRC) among others. Note that the Strain/Stock Designation column links to strain information at the repository site; the Holder Site column links to the holder for questions or orders; farther right, the Allele Symbol column links to the Phenotypic Allele Detail page in MGI.

  5. Click on any link in the Strain/Stock Designation column for more information about a specific strain.
