How do I find available strains carrying a mutation in a specific gene?


Overview
You can use the International Mouse Strain Resource (IMSR) to locate resources holding particular mouse strains or stocks.
Accessing the IMSR
On most MGI pages, you can access the IMSR from the MGI navigation bar as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the IMSR in a new window or tab.


Example 1. Finding suppliers of mice with mutations in gene Rag2
  1. Click Search IMSR.
  1. Uncheck boxes next to:
    Cryopreserved Ovaries
    Cryopreserved Embryos
    Cryopreserved/Freeze-Dried Sperm
  2. Check the box next to:
    Live Mice

  3. Type rag2 in the Gene or Allele Symbol/Name text box.

  4. Click Search.
  1. Click on a link in the Strain/Stock Designation column to request a specific strain from the holder.
