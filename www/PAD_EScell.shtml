How do I find an ES cell line?


Overview
You can use the International Mouse Strain Resource (IMSR) to locate resources holding particular mouse strains or stocks.
Accessing the IMSR
On most MGI pages, you can access the IMSR from the MGI navigation bar as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the IMSR in a new window.


Example 1. Finding suppliers of ES cell lines with mutations in gene Acadm
  1. Click Search IMSR.
  1. Uncheck boxes next to:
    Live Mice
    Cryopreserved Ovaries
    Cryopreserved Embryos
    Cryopreserved/Freeze-Dried Sperm
  2. Check the box next to:
    ES cell lines

  3. Type acadm in the Gene or Allele Symbol/Name text box.

  4. Click Search.
  1. Click on a link in the Strain/Stock Designation column to request a specific ES cell line from the holder.
Example 2. Finding all ES cell lines in MGI with mutations in gene Acadm
  1. Open the homepages/index.shtml"target = "blank">MGI home page in a new window.

  2. Type acadm into the search box.

  3. Click Search.
  1. The Search Results page lists symbols and names containing acadm. Click the link to WIFetch?page=markerDetail&key=25"target = "blank">Acadm.
  1. Scroll down the Acadm Gene Detail page to the Other database links section. Click the link to WIFetch?page=GTSummary&key=25"target = "blank">All 30... to view a list of all gene traps associated with Acadm.
  1. Click a link in the Gene Trap column to view additional details at NCBI.
