How do I find an ES cell line?

  1. To find embryonic stem (ES) cell lines, click here to open the International Mouse Strain Resource (IMSR) search form in a new window. The IMSR is located in the toolbar, as shown below.

  2. For Strain/Stock State(s), uncheck boxes next to Live Mice, Cryopreserved Ovaries, Cryopreserved Embryos, and Cryopreserved/Freeze-Dried Sperm.

  3. Check the box next to ES cell lines.

  4. In the Gene or Allele Symbol/Name text box, type pax.

  5. Click the Search button.

  6. The International Mouse Strain Resource contains information from a number of different repositories including the Texas Institute for Genomic Medicine (TIGM), the Mutant Mouse Regional Resources Center (MMRRC), and others. Note that the Strain/Stock Designation column links to strain information at the repository site; the Holder Site column links to the holder for questions or orders; farther right, the Allele Symbol column links to the Phenotypic Allele Detail page in MGI.

  7. Click on any link in the Strain/Stock Designation column for more information about a specific ES cell line.
