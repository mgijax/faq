How do I find expression data for a tissue during development?

  1. Click searches/anatdict_form.shtml"TARGET = "BLANK">here to open the Mouse Anatomical Dictionary Browser in a new window. The Mouse Anatomical Dictionary Browser is located beneath the Search menu in the toolbar, as shown below.

  2. Type the name of the tissue into the Query text box. As an example, type heart.

  3. Click the Search button.

  4. Your return will list all the developmental (Theiler) stages that contain terms that match the query. As an example, click on heart in Theiler stage 17.

  5. Click on show all to see all expression results annotated to the heart and its substructures. To further refine your query, click the link to refine search.

  6. Once the query results have been returned, click on any link in the Result Details column to view details from assays that examined expression in the heart in Theiler stage 17.
