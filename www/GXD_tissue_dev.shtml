How do I find expression data for a tissue during development?
Overview
You can use The Mouse Anatomical Dictionary Browser to find anatomical terms of most interest to you, either by browsing the hierarchy of anatomical structures or by searching for specific terms. You can then use the links provided to find the expression results associated with those terms.
Accessing the Mouse Anatomical Dictionary Browser
On most MGI pages, you can access the Mouse Anatomical Dictionary Browser from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

Example 1. Finding expression results for the heart in Theiler stage 17
  1. Type heart into the Query text box.

  2. Click the Search button.
  1. Click on searches/anatdict.cgi?id=2167' onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('searches/anatdict.cgi?id=2167'); return false;"> heart in Theiler stage 17.
  1. Click on show all to see all expression results annotated to the heart and its substructures.
  1. Click data in the Result Details column to view details from assays that examined expression in the heart in Theiler stage 17.
