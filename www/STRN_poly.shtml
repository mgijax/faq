How do I find strains with RFLP/PCR polymorphisms in a particular gene?


Overview
You can use the RFLP/PCR Polymorphism Query Form to compare polymorphisms in mouse strains by locus symbol, chromosome, or chromosomal region. You can also list all polymorphisms for one or more strains by locus symbol, chromosome, or chromosomal region.
Accessing the RFLP/PCR Polymorphism Query Form
On most MGI pages, you can access the RFLP/PCR Polymorphism Query Form from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the searches/polymorphism_form.shtml' onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('searches/polymorphism_form.shtml')"> RFLP/PCR Polymorphism Query Form in a new window.


Example 1. Finding polymorphisms associated gene Cyp1a1
  1. Type cyp1a1 into the Locus Symbol/Name text box.

  2. Click Search.
  1. Click a searches/polymorphism.cgi?_RFLV_key=18023'> PCR link in the Type column to view details about alleles, relative fragment sizes and strains.
  1. Click a link in the searches/probe.cgi?34750'> Probe column to retrieve further details, including primer sequences in many cases.

  2. For RFLPs, return to step 3 above and click an searches/polymorphism.cgi?_RFLV_key=1794'> RFLP link in the Type column to view details.
