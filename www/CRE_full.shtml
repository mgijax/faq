How do I get a full list of all transgenes and knock-ins that express recombinase?
Overview
You can use the tools listed under "Access Data" to find transgenes that express recombinase.
Retrieve All Alleles
On most MGI pages, you can access the Recombinase (cre) Specificity page from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the recombinase.shtml' onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('recombinase.shtml'); return false;"> Recombinase (cre) Specificity page in a new window. Scroll down this page for further instructions. The Retrieve All Alleles section gives you access to HTML and tab-delimited lists of all recombinase strains present in the database.


Example 1. HTML version
  1. Click the link to HTML version.
  1. In the page that appears, click an allele symbol to go to the Transgene Detail page for more information.

Example 2. Tab-delimited version
  1. Click the link to Tab-delimited version.
  1. A tab-delimited version of the list appears.
