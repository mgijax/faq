How do I view annotations for mouse/human/rat ortholog sets?

  1. Click homepages/index.shtml"TARGET = "BLANK">here to open the MGI home page in a new window. To view annotations for mouse/human/rat ortholog sets, select Prototype Tools from the More Resources menu, as shown below.

  2. On the Prototype Tools page, scroll to the bottom and click the link to GOgraphEX - GO Graphical Explorer.

  3. On the GOgraphEX page, click the link to GO Comparative Graphs: mouse-human-rat experimental annotations.

  4. Click the link to Compare mouse-human-rat GO annotations.

  5. Click on the letter S to view a list of gene symbols beginning with this character.

  6. Scroll down the page to gene symbol Slc22a6.

  7. Click the link to Graph: Slc22a6 at far right to view a graph showing relationships or "paths" from the molecular function, cellular component and biological process ontologies, to the lowest level terms such as sodium-independent organic anion transport.
