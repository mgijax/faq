Phenotype information is now displayed in two ways. The new Phenotype Summary matrix allows easy comparison of phenotypes across genotypes. The Phenotypic Data by Genotype table preserves the original display format allowing you to focus on the details of a specific genotype. See the figures below for a quick tour.

Phenotype Summary

  1. Click show to display all mammalian phenotype terms linked to at least one genotype.

  2. Click on a genotype abbreviation, e.g. hm1, to open a new window that contains phenotype and disease model information for this genotype. This information is repeated below in the Phenotypic Data by Genotype section.

  3. Click on a right arrow next to an affected system to display all mammalian phenotype terms in that system linked to at least one genotype.

Phenotypic Data by Genotype

  1. Click show to display all phenotype and disease model annotations for all genotypes.

  2. Click on the right arrow next to a genotype to display all phenotype and disease model annotations for that genotype.

For more information see Understanding Phenotypic Allele Detail Pages.
