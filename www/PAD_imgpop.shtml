About phenotype images

Whenever possible the text annotations of phenotypes in MGI are complemented by images illustrating the phenotype (photographs, histology, x-rays, etc.). Images are associated with the genotypes of the mice displayed and are found on the relevant allele detail page(s). Phenotype images come either from journal articles (with permission of the journal) or through contributed submissions.
  • The image shown here is of a mouse homozygous for Ednrbs. Note that additional images of mice carrying this allele are available by clicking on the "Show the 4 image(s) involving this allele".
