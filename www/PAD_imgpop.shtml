Phenotype Images in MGI

Whenever possible the text annotations of phenotypes in MGI are complemented by images illustrating the phenotype (photographs, histology, x-rays, etc.). Images may be found on the relevant allele detail page(s). Phenotype images come either from journal articles (with permission of the journal) or through contributed submissions.
