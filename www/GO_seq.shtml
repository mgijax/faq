How do I obtain FASTA sequence files for genes with GO annotations in common?

  1. Click here to open the Mouse Sequence Query Form in a new window. The Mouse Sequence Query Form is located beneath the search menu as shown below.

  2. Find the first section of the form, entitled Sequence attributes. For Sequence Type, select Polypeptide from the pick list.

  3. Scroll down the page to the Gene Ontology (GO) classifications section located near the center of the form.

  4. Type the term histone deacetylation in the Gene Classifications text box.

  5. Click the Search button.

  6. The search will return all polypeptide sequences annotated to GO term "histone deacetylation".

  7. To select all sequences, click the Select all button located at upper left.

  8. Find the drop-down menu in the upper center of the page that allows you to download in FASTA format or forward to MouseBLAST.

  9. Click the Go button to download the selected sequences in FASTA format.
