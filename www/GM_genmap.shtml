How do I create a genome map from a genomic interval?
Overview
Mouse Genome Browser graphically displays genes, alleles, phenotypes, QTL and SNPs on mouse chromosomes.
Accessing Mouse Genome Browser
On most MGI pages, you can access Mouse Genome Browser from the Search menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open Mouse Genome Browser in a new window.


Example 1. Displaying targeted alleles, VEGA transcripts, QTL and a variety of phenotypes on Chromosome 7 between 101 and 103 Mbp
  1. Change the text in the Landmark or Region text box to 7:101000000..103000000 to view chromosome 7 from 101Mbp to 103Mbp.

  2. Click Search.
  1. Scroll down the page to the Tracks section.

  2. Check the boxes next to the following:
    Alleles
    Alleles_targeted
    Genes
    VEGA transcripts
    Phenotypes
    Behavior_neurological
    Digestive_alimentary
    Growth_size
    Homeostasis_metabolism
    Immune_system
    QTL

  3. Click Update Image.
  1. The Details section displays QTL for this region of chromosome 7. Beneath the QTL, phenotype terms are listed at left, and associated transcripts modeled to the right of these terms.

  2. Click on a transcript to view a list of alleles.
