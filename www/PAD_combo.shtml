How do I find mutations that cause a specific combination of phenotypes?

  1. Click here to go to the Phenotypes and Alleles Query Form. The Phenotypes and Alleles Query Form is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. In the Phenotype/Disease text box near the top of the page, type hyperglycemia AND glucosuria.

  3. Click the Search button.

  4. The results page lists all alleles where both terms appear as a Mammalian Phenotype Ontology term, a synonym to one of these terms, a disease term or synonym, or in the notes describing a phenotype.

  5. Click any allele symbol in the first column of the search results to view greater detail about an allele, including descriptions of phenotypes and any associated diseases. Note that the precise search terms entered may not appear in the phenotype descriptions for an allele. These search terms may have been captured as synonyms during the search, and be represented by different terms than those originally entered.
