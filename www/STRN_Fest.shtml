How do I find characteristics of a specific strain in M. Festing's Inbred Strains of Mice and Rats?

  1. Click here to open Dr. Michael Festing's Inbred Strains of Mice and Rats in a new window. Festing's Inbred Strains of Mice and Rats is located beneath the Search menu as shown below.

  2. Type C57BL/6 into the text box and hit the Return or Enter key on your keyboard.

  3. The results will list all strains that include any mention of C57BL/6 in their descriptions. Click the C57BL link in the list of strains returned to read about strain C57BL and its substrains, including C57BL/6.
