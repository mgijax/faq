How do I see a list of all pathways in MouseCyc?
Overview
You can use the Biochemical Pathways page to access mouse genetic and genomic data in the context of physiological and cellular processes.
Accessing the Biochemical Pathways page
On most MGI pages, you can access the Biochemical Pathways page by clicking the appropriate tab as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the pathways.shtml' onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('pathways.shtml'); return false;"> Biochemical Pathways page in a new window.


Example 1. Viewing a list of all pathways in MouseCyc
  1. Click the link to the Browse the pathway ontology in the MouseCyc database.
  1. Click the Expand All button to view a list of all pathways.
