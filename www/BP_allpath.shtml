How do I see a list of all pathways in MouseCyc?

  1. Click here to go to the Biochemical Pathways page. This page may be accessed by clicking the Biochemical Pathways link on the MGI home page, or by clicking the tab near the top center of other MGI pages.

  2. Find the box labeled What can I do here? located at left. Click the link to Browse the pathway ontology in the MouseCyc database.

  3. Click the Expand all button to view a list of all pathways.
