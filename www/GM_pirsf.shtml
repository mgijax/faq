How do I search for genes sharing a specific protein domain?

  1. Click WIFetch?page=pirsfVocab&subset=A"TARGET = "BLANK">here to open the Protein Superfamily Browser in a new window. The Protein Superfamily Browser is located beneath the Search menu in the toolbar, as shown below.

  2. As an example, type potassium channels in the text box.

  3. Click the Search button.

  4. Click the link to potassium channel protein Drk1 from the list of genes in this superfamily, along with human and rat orthologs and protein sequence IDs.
