How do I find genomic or cDNA clones?

  1. Click here to open the Molecular Probes and Clones Query Form in a new window. The Molecular Probes and Clones Query Form is located beneath the Search menu, as shown below.

  2. For Gene/Marker Symbol/Name, change "contains" to "=" in the drop-down menu and type Whrn in the text box.

  3. Find the Probe/Clone attributes section, select "genomic" from the Sequence Type drop-down menu.

  4. Click the Search button.

  5. Click on the link to MGI:2785018.

  6. On the page that follows, copy the ID number RP23-340H1.

  7. Go to the BACPAC Resources Center at http://bacpac.chori.org/ to order this clone.

  8. For cDNA clones, return to the Molecular Probes and Clones Query Form.

  9. Type Whrn in the Gene/Marker Symbol/Name text box and select "cDNA" for sequence type.

  10. Detail pages for cDNA clones may contain direct links to IMAGE or RIKEN clone suppliers. To order RIKEN clones specifically, visit DNAForm at http://www.dnaform.jp/products/clone_e.html.
