How do I join or unsubscribe from MGI list or MGI Technical List?
Overview
You can subscribe to MGI list and MGI Technical List from the MGI website. Once you have subscribed, you can submit questions and receive responses from other members of the list.
Accessing the MGI E-mail List Service
On most MGI pages, you can access the MGI E-mail List Service from the More Resources menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the /lists/lists.shtml" onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('/lists/lists.shtml'); return false;"> MGI E-mail List Service page in a new window. Scroll down this page for further instructions.


Example 1. Joining MGI-list
  1. Type your name and e-mail address into the text boxes.

  2. Check the box next to mgi-list.

  3. Click the Submit button.
