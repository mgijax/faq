How do I join or unsubscribe from MGI-list and Phenome-list?

  1. Click homepages/index.shtml"TARGET = "BLANK">here to open the MGI home page in a new window. To join or unsubscribe from MGI-list or Phenome-list, select Research Community E-Mail Lists from the More Resources menu, as shown below.

  2. To subscribe, type your name and e-mail address into the text boxes.

  3. Check a box next to mgi-list or phenome-list and click the Submit button.

  4. To unsubscribe, click the link to Enter MGI-LIST or Enter PHENOME-LIST.

  5. Type your e-mail address and password into the text boxes and click the button to enter.

  6. Click the Leave(unsubscribe) button.
