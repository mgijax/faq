How do I join or unsubscribe from MGI-list and Phenome-list?


Overview
You can subscribe to MGI-list and Phenome-list from the MGI website. Once you have subscribed, you can submit questions and receive responses from other members of the list.
Accessing the MGI E-mail List Service
On most MGI pages, you can access the MGI E-mail List Service from the More Resources menu as shown in the image at right.

For this tutorial, open the /lists/lists.shtml" onClick="javascript:openNewWindow('/lists/lists.shtml'); return false;"> MGI E-mail List Service page in a new window.


Example 1. Joining MGI-list
  1. Type your name and e-mail address into the text boxes.

  2. Check the box next to mgi-list.

  3. Click the Submit button.
